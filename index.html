FlowHaus Credibility Audit

A quick diagnostic to evaluate a website’s trust signals and highlight conversion risks.

  1. Enter a website
  2. Answer 12 quick credibility questions
  3. Get a score and practical improvement areas

This tool provides directional insight only and does not constitute legal, SEO, or compliance advice.

Clarity

1) Within 5 seconds, is it obvious who you help and what you do?

2) Is there a clear value-driven headline (not just a slogan)?

3) Do you offer one primary service instead of many competing options?

Proof

4) Do you show testimonials, reviews, or client quotes?

5) Do you show examples, case studies, or a portfolio?

6) Do you display trust markers (years, certifications, stats, logos)?

Design Trust

7) Does the site feel modern and clean on mobile?

8) Is branding consistent across fonts, colors, and visuals?

9) Does everything load correctly without typos or broken layouts?

Conversion Path

10) Is there one clear call-to-action on the homepage?

11) Is it easy to understand the next step or pricing?

12) Is contacting you fast and frictionless?
